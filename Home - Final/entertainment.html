Today's Headlines

Actor Kevin Clark dies after being hit by car while riding his bicycle in US

Actor Kevin Clark, who played drummer Freddy "Spazzy McGee" Jones in the 2003 movie 'School of Rock' with Jack Black, died after he was hit by a car while riding his bicycle in US' Chicago. Authorities said emergency responders found Clark, 32, partially under the car. "Devastating news. Kevin is gone. Way too soon," Black wrote on Instagram.

Brad Pitt wins joint custody of 5 kids with his ex-wife Angelina Jolie

Brad Pitt has been granted joint custody of his children with his ex-wife Angelina Jolie after a private judge that the two actors hired to oversee their divorce sided with him. Pitt and Jolie are parents to 17-year-old Pax, 16-year-old Zahara, 14-year-old Shiloh and 12-year-old twins Vivienne and Knox. Their oldest, 19-year-old Maddox, is not subject to the custody decision.

Anurag Kashyap undergoes angioplasty after complaining of mild pain in chest

Filmmaker Anurag Kashyap underwent an angioplasty over the weekend in a Mumbai hospital after he complained of mild pain in chest. The director's spokesperson confirmed the news of the director undergoing surgery to ABP News and added that he is currently recuperating. Kashyap has been advised a week's rest before he can resume work.

Sanjay Dutt receives golden visa for the UAE

Sanjay Dutt on Wednesday revealed that he has received the golden visa for the United Arab Emirates (UAE). Taking to Twitter, the 61-year-old actor shared a picture of himself holding his passport. He also thanked the UAE government for granting him the golden visa. According to Gulf News, a golden visa is a 10-year-long residency permit.

Kartik Aaryan's 'Dhamaka' to release in September: Reports

Actor Kartik Aaryan's upcoming film 'Dhamaka' will release on an OTT platform in September this year, according to reports. "The initial plan was to unveil it in June, but for now they have decided on September," Pinkvilla said quoting a source. Directed by Ram Madhvani, the action-thriller film also stars Mrunal Thakur and Amruta Subhash.
